About Us

A visit to The Sewing Shed will be a memorable experience. We have a wealth of knowledge within the Team and pride ourselves on our Customer Service. We are happy to share our expertise with our customers. We welcome visitors from all over the world; and accommodate tour groups. The Sewing Shed offers on location classes for any size groups.

Welcome to The Sewing Shed

The Sewing Shed, The cutest little quilt shop in Ireland, is located on one of the most iconic peninsulas in Ireland; The Dingle Peninsula. Tucked away in rural Kerry, this little gem has been likened to Aladdins Cave. Packed full with Fabrics, notions and everything you need to create beautiful quilts and other stitched projects. In the hub of the community the shop provides social interaction on a weekly basis for people of all ages, and caters for all skillsets from Beginners to the more advanced.

Brush up on existing techniques

The sit and sew sessions run every week and are open with no requirement to pre book. With a minimum of two sit and sews a week there is a time to suit all. 10 week classes run during term time and we offer beginners, intermediate and advanced classes throughout the year. Check out the calendar to see what is happening now and in the coming months.

Fun Trips Away
Meet Amazing People
Show and tell

We have been specialising in Quilting Retreats for a number of years at The Sewing Shed. At each retreat we offer a different project or you can just choose to work on your own creation with our guidance. Each one differs with location and duration specific to our hand picked locations. We ensure uninterrupted sewing time, with food, beverages and guaranteed memorable time away. Over the years we have retreated to France, Germany, The U.K and across Ireland. Keep an eye out on our social channels for information on our upcoming retreats!

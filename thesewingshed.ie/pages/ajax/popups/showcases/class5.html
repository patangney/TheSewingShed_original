Themed Weekends

Themed weekend are extremely popular and a lot of fun. Taking place on Thursday, Friday and Saturday we work on a specific project over the entire weekend. These weekends make our famous classes available to everyone and we have a list of BnB and air BnB accommodations just waiting to look after you. During the weekend we also organise an evening out for a meal as a group so we can relax and chat for a few hours.

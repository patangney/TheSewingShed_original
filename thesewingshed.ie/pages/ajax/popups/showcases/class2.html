Beginner and Intermediate

Our intermediate/beginners class run during term times on a Monday or Tuesday evening 7.30- 9.30pm. You will make a quilt during the class, each evening you will learn and work on a new technique. Your skills will with develop and progress during the ten weeks. These classes are available 3 times a year. January, April and September.

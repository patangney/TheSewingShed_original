Block of the Month

Each year we have a BOM- This is a quilt that you make a block each month for a year. For 2018 we have a quilt called the ‘Ring of Kerry’ The quilt is made up from 13 blocks, and will start in September 2017 and run for 15 months. You can do the BOM class at The Sewing Shed once a month, or you can do it by email. There will be you tube tutorials to support the monthly instructions. You can join the BOM any time, even after it has started

Sarah Noonan

Teacher

Sarah Noonan is a teacher at the Sewing Shed, for many years Sarah has played an integral part in classes and workshops and the running of the shop. Her patience and caring attitude has introduced many people into patchwork and quilting over the last 6 years. Sarah teaches from beginners level upwards and is fabulous at guiding those who have never sewn anything before.


Sarah has also got a fabulous sense of colour, and is always on hand to to help pick the right fabric. She is a great asset to the team. Sarah is also part of the IQS along with 13 other quilters from around Ireland. The participants of Irish Quilters Showcase each year make a quilt based on a theme. The quilts are then exhibited at different locations around Ireland and the UK
